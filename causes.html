Causes

Nurturing Anticipation

In a promising interaction, our co-founder Penina Odhiambo recently convened with our upcoming cohort, slated to embark on their coding journey this December. During the productive meeting, Penina attentively grasped their aspirations, all the while providing inspiration and issuing a compelling call to recognize the significance of education.

Empowering Innovator

Wesakania Davis, the founder and driving force behind Wessklink Mashinani, exudes an infectious enthusiasm that fuels her mission of community empowerment. With unwavering commitment and dedication, Davis is reshaping local initiatives, infusing them with innovation and boundless energy, and inspiring a brighter future for all.

Bridging the Digital Divide

In a dynamic session, the Wessklink Mashinani team engaged with students, forging a collaborative path to tackle the digital divide in the context of pressing climate change challenges in line with vision 2030. Through this synergy, a powerful fusion of ideas emerges, uniting technology and environmental action for a sustainable future.

Code for All

By joining forces with local communities, Wessklink Mashinani team is democratizing coding education in rural schools in line with new the Competence Based Curriculum (CBC). This partnership dismantles barriers by providing essential digital skills and paving the way for inclusive technological progress in underserved rural areas in the entire Machakos County.

Village Renaissance

Wessklink Mashinani sparks rural potential by involving youth in technology, coding, climate change, girl education and environmental conservation. Empowering village youth as visionary catalysts, they cultivate seeds of progress, nurturing a holistic transformation that surpasses obstacles and propels communities towards a more equitable, radiant future.

Tech-Infused Learning for Young Minds

Wessklink Mashinani pioneers the integration of technology into the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). Empowering young learners with digital tools and skills of coding, they redefine education, fostering a dynamic and interactive learning experience that prepares them for a tech-driven future.
